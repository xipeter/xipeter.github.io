# Passport Photo Sizer

A free tool to help you crop and resize passport photos to meet official dimension requirements. This tool assists with the technical aspect of preparing photos for passport, visa, and ID applications.

## What This Tool Does

- **Dimension presets** - Pre-configured sizes for major passport agencies
- **Crop guidance** - Visual guides to help you crop to exact dimensions
- **Requirement checks** - Basic validation of photo dimensions
- **Print guidance** - Tips for printing at correct resolution

## Supported Presets

| Region | Dimensions | Use Case |
|--------|------------|----------|
| US | 2x2 inch | US Passport, TSA |
| UK | 35x45mm | UK Passport |
| EU | 35x45mm | Schengen, EU passports |
| Canada | 50x70mm | Canadian Passport |
| Australia | 35x45mm | Australian Passport |

## How to Use

1. Select your target country/agency from the presets
2. Upload or paste your photo
3. Use the crop tool to adjust the framing
4. Preview the cropped result
5. Download or save for printing

## Important Notes

**This tool helps with dimensions only.** To be accepted, your photo must also meet requirements for:

- **Background** - Usually plain white or light colored
- **Lighting** - Even, without shadows
- **Expression** - Neutral face, eyes open
- **Glasses** - Usually not allowed (some exceptions)
- **Head position** - Centered, looking straight

Check your specific passport agency's website for complete requirements.

## Disclaimer

This tool provides dimension guidance only. We cannot guarantee acceptance by any passport agency. Always verify with your local passport authority for current requirements.
