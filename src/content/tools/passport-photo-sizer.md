# Free Online Passport Photo Maker

Make passport photos at home — for free. Upload your photo, and this tool will remove the background, resize to the exact dimensions your country requires, and arrange copies on a sheet ready to print.

## How It Works

1. **Upload any photo** — a selfie or a photo taken against a wall works fine
2. **Pick your country** — the tool knows the exact size for US, UK, EU, Canada, Australia, and China passports
3. **One click to process** — background is removed and replaced with white, photo is cropped and resized to official dimensions
4. **Choose how many copies** — arrange 1 to 8 photos on a single sheet (4×6, A4, or US Letter paper)
5. **Download and print** — get a high-resolution PNG ready to print at home or at a photo kiosk

## Tips for Best Results

- **Use good lighting** — even, natural light without harsh shadows on your face
- **Plain background** — a white or light-colored wall works best for background removal
- **Face the camera directly** — eyes open, neutral expression, no glasses if possible
- **High resolution** — use your phone's main camera, not the selfie camera, for better quality
- **Leave space around your head** — the tool will crop to the right ratio, so don't crop too tight

## Why Use This Instead of a Photo Shop?

- **Free** — photo shops typically charge $10-15 for passport photos
- **Instant** — no waiting, no driving, no appointments
- **Private** — your photo stays on your device, never uploaded anywhere
- **Unlimited retakes** — keep trying until you're happy with the result
- **Print anywhere** — download and print at home, at a pharmacy, or at a kiosk

## Important

This tool handles **dimensions and background only**. Your passport agency may also require specific head size ratios, eye positions, and other criteria. Always verify the full requirements with your country's passport authority before submitting photos.
