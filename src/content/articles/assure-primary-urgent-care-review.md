# Assure Primary and Urgent Care: A Walk-In Visit with Our Son

On March 26, I took my son to Assure Primary and Urgent Care after a worrying coughing episode. His cough had been severe for a few days, and that morning we noticed his phlegm had a pinkish tint. We couldn't tell whether it was blood, and to be safe we decided to get him seen the same day.

## Why We Chose Assure Primary and Urgent Care

Friends had recommended CVS Minute Clinic, but the nearest location was a bit of a drive. Assure Primary and Urgent Care was close to home, had strong reviews, and accepted walk-ins — so we headed there instead.

Their walk-in hours ended at 5:30 PM, and we arrived a few minutes past the cutoff. After a brief conversation at the front desk, the staff still agreed to see us. Small moments like that matter when you have a sick kid and no appointment.

## The Visit

- **Wait time** was reasonable — not too short, not too long.
- **The physician was patient**, took time to listen, and explained things clearly.
- **Tests and treatment** included the right diagnostics and an on-site nebulizer session.
- **Prescriptions** were sent in so we could start treatment right away.

By the next day, his cough had improved significantly. As an unexpected bonus, his seasonal allergy symptoms also eased up noticeably in the days that followed.

![Prescriptions from the visit: Children's Loratadine oral solution and Albuterol Sulfate HFA inhalation aerosol](/articles/assure-urgent-care-prescriptions.webp)

## Cost

The pricing was very reasonable — much more approachable than what we'd expected from urgent care in this area. After insurance, our final out-of-pocket cost was **$22.65** on a $515 billed amount — covering the office visit, a SARS-CoV-2/Influenza A&B test, a Strep A assay, and the in-office nebulizer (airway inhalation) treatment.

![Claim cost breakdown: $515 billed, $22.65 out-of-pocket after plan discount and insurance](/articles/assure-urgent-care-claim.webp)

## Verdict

If you're looking for a nearby walk-in clinic with patient, attentive staff and fair pricing, I strongly recommend Assure Primary and Urgent Care. Great experience for our family, and we would absolutely go back.

# 推荐 Assure Primary and Urgent Care —— 一次 Walk-in 就诊经历

3 月 26 日，我带儿子去 Assure Primary and Urgent Care 看病。他前几天咳嗽得比较严重，当天早上我们发现他的痰带一点粉红色，不确定是不是血。为了安全起见，我们决定当天就带他去 urgent care 看一下。

## 为什么选择这家

之前朋友推荐过 CVS Minute Clinic，但最近的一家离我们有点远。Assure Primary and Urgent Care 离家比较近，评价也不错，而且接受 walk-in，所以我们就直接过去了。

他们的 walk-in 接诊时间到下午 5:30 结束，我们到的时候已经超过了几分钟。和前台沟通后，他们还是接受了我们的就诊——带着生病的孩子临时赶过去，这种灵活性真的很难得。

## 就诊体验

- **等待时间适中**，不会太久也不会太赶。
- **医生非常耐心**，认真听我们描述症状，也把病情解释得很清楚。
- **检查和治疗**都很到位，还当场做了雾化。
- **开的药**可以及时拿到，让孩子第一时间开始治疗。

第二天孩子的咳嗽就明显好转了。更意外的是，接下来几天他的过敏症状也跟着缓解了不少。

![从诊所拿到的药品：儿童氯雷他定口服液和沙丁胺醇吸入气雾剂](/articles/assure-urgent-care-prescriptions.webp)

## 费用

费用也非常友好，比我们预期的 urgent care 价格要合理很多。总账单 $515，经过保险和 plan discount 之后，我们实际自付只有 **$22.65**——包含门诊费、流感/新冠检测、链球菌检测和当场的雾化治疗。

![医疗账单明细：$515 总账单，经保险处理后自付 $22.65](/articles/assure-urgent-care-claim.webp)

## 总结

如果你在附近找一家接受 walk-in、医护耐心、价格合理的诊所，我非常推荐 Assure Primary and Urgent Care。我们全家的体验都很好，以后还会再去。
